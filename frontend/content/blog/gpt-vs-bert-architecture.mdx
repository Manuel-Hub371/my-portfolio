Choosing the right transformer architecture depends on your task. Here's a concise comparison.

## BERT (Encoder-Only)

- **Training**: Masked language modeling — predict hidden tokens.
- **Best for**: Classification, NER, embeddings, retrieval encoders.
- **Inference**: Bidirectional context in one pass.

## GPT (Decoder-Only)

- **Training**: Causal language modeling — predict next token.
- **Best for**: Text generation, chat, code completion, agents.
- **Inference**: Autoregressive, left-to-right.

## T5 / BART (Encoder-Decoder)

- **Best for**: Summarization, translation, seq2seq tasks.

## Practical Guidance

| Task | Recommended |
|------|-------------|
| Semantic search encoder | BERT / E5 |
| Chat / agents | GPT-class |
| Summarization | T5 / fine-tuned GPT |

Modern LLMs (GPT-4, Claude) are decoder-only at scale, while RAG systems often pair a BERT-style encoder for retrieval with a GPT decoder for generation.
